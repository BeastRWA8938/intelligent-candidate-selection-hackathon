REDROB CANDIDATE SIEVE DETAILED FINDINGS
========================================

1. SIEVE IMPLEMENTATION DETAILS
-------------------------------
The sieve is implemented in a reusable module `scratch/sieve.py`. It exposes two primary functions:
- `is_honeypot(candidate, current_time, yoe_threshold, dur_threshold, expert_zero_threshold)`:
  Checks date-duration logic and expert zero-duration skills:
  * Rule 1 (YoE Mismatch): Stated Years of Experience exceeds the calendar span of jobs (first job start to 2026-06-18) by > 0.5 years.
  * Rule 2 (Job Duration Mismatch): Stated job duration in months exceeds the calendar span of that job (start date to end date, or to 2026-06-18 if current) by > 3 months.
  * Rule 3 (Expert Zero-Duration Skills): Candidate has >= 1 skill with "expert" proficiency and 0 months of stated duration.
- `is_disqualified(candidate)`:
  Checks the disqualification logic:
  * Check 1 (Bad Titles): Candidate's current title is in the set of irrelevant trap titles (BAD_TITLES).
  * Check 2 (Consulting Only): Candidate's entire career history consists exclusively of IT consulting/services firms (CONSULTING_COMPANIES).
